Vowels and niggahīta as diacritical marks, except at the beginning of a word.
IAST āīū kakākikīkukūkekokaṃ
Keyboard aAiIuU kkAkIkIIkUkUUkEkOkM
provident aAiIuU kkAkIkIIkUkUUkEkOkM
除单字的开头外，元音以变音符号呈现。

Consanants (ṅ and ṇ share same key code 'N', ṅ and ṃ are not shown as an alphabet)
IAST khghchjh ṭṭhḍḍhñ dhthṇphbh ḷ ṅkṅkhṅgṅgh
Keyboard KGCJ VWFQY DTNPB L NXkNXKNXgNXG
provident KGCJ VWFQY DTNPB L NXkNXKNXgNXG
ṅ及ṇ共用代码'N', 辅音ṅ及ṃ不以字母形态出现。

Sequence of consanants must be separated by 'X' to construct a ligature.
For unknown sequence, a small + is placed in the middle. gXt => gXt
IAST kkhagghaccha jjhaṭṭaṭṭhaḍḍaḍḍha ññaddhatthaṇṇa pphabbha
Keyboard kXKgXGcXC jXJVXVVXWFXFFXQ YXYdXDtXTNXN pXPbXB
provident kXKgXGcXC jXJVXVVXWFXFFXQ YXYdXDtXTNXN pXPbXB
连续辅音必须以X隔开，以形成合字。
如果不是字体能够识别的的连音，X会变成小号的 +，例gXt =>gXt

Syllables are more cohesive, taking less brain power to scan and parse. No more ambiguity between "ha" and other aspirating alphabets, searching "hala" will never return "khala", "phala" ,"thala".
IAST buddhaṃdhammaṃsaṅghaṃavyākatapaṭhamaṃ jhānaṃsotāpanna
Keyboard bUdXDMDmXmMsNXGMavXyAktpWmM JAnMsOtApnXn
provident bUdXDMDmXmMsNXGMavXyAktpWmM JAnMsOtApnXn
字母与音节的对应清楚，识别更省力。"ha" 也不再会和其他送气音搞混。 比如检索“hala”，不再会返回 phala, khala, thala
